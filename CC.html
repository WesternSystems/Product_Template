Celestia Callisto is Europe's leading specialist in cryogenic and ambient Low Noise Amplifiers (LNAs) for satellite ground stations, deep space communications, radio astronomy and VLBI. With over 25 years of experience and hundreds of units deployed at ESA, CNES, ISRO, BKG, NASA DSN partner stations and 70+ ground stations worldwide, Callisto pushes receiver sensitivity to the fundamental limits of physics.
